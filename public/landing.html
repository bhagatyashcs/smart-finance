Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an account to use this?

No! You can use the dashboard as a guest. However, creating a free account allows you to securely save your data to MongoDB so it doesn't disappear when you close the browser.

Are my financial details safe?

Yes. If you create an account, your data is securely stored in a database with industry-standard JWT encryption protecting your session.

Why is Crypto tax so high?

Under Section 115BBH of the Income Tax Act, Virtual Digital Assets are taxed at a flat 30% without allowing deductions for losses.

Smart Money Facts

The 50/30/20 Rule

A popular budgeting method: spend 50% of your after-tax pay on needs, 30% on wants, and put 20% toward savings or paying off debt.

Power of Compounding

Albert Einstein reportedly called compound interest the "eighth wonder of the world." Reinvesting your profits causes your wealth to grow exponentially over time.

LTCG Exemption

In India, if you hold stocks for over a year, the first ₹1,25,000 of your profit is completely tax-free. Patience pays off!
